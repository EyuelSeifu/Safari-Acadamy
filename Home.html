About Us

Hello, It is Safari Acadamy and it is

Safari Academy was established in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia in 2005 G.C having a motto of “ Your kids our kids!”. At the time of establishment, Safari Academy began with 8 staff members and registered only 107 students from KG to Grade 2. Due to the teachers and supporting staff members strong effort, the academy currently has 2 Kindergartens, 3 primary schools, and 1 secondary and college preparatory campuses and serves nearly 6,000 students, more than 560 teachers and supportive staffs.
